Mint Tea

Ingredients

  • Mint Leaves
  • Lemon
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Take Some Mint leaves in a small bowl.
  2. Cut the lemon into circles.
  3. Add them to the mint bowl.
  4. Muddle both lemon and mint leaves to together.
  5. Take the mixture out in glass.
  6. Add water into that glass.
  7. Stir it well before straing.
  8. Strain it and ice to it.

Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Red pepper

Steps

  1. Break the eggs big bowl.
  2. Add milk,salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the pan.
  4. Put some oil.
  5. Beat the egg mixture.
  6. Add red bell peppers and egg mixture to the heated pan and stir it .
  7. Plate the eggs and Serve it hot.

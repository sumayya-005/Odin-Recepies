Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  1. Chicken Wings
  2. Flour
  3. Soy Sauce
  4. salt and Pepper
  5. BBQ sauce
  6. Garlic
  7. Oil

Procedure

  • Wash meat with water thoroughly.
  • Take them in a bowl.
  • Add salt,pepper and soy sauce.
  • Marinate for about 30 min.
  • Add flour after marination.
  • Deep fry them in oil and take out in plate
  • Next add BBQ Sauce to the fried wings and serve them hot.

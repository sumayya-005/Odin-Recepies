STRAWBERRY CAKE

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Strawberries
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Baking soda
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in big bowl.
  2. Pour all wet ingredients in bowl.
  3. Preheat the oven 180 degrees.
  4. Pour wet ones into dry bowl.
  5. Mix well before baking.
  6. Put cake mixture in a cake mould.
  7. Bake the cake in the oven for about45 min.
  8. After cooling down decorate with Strawberries.
